RICK ASTLEY

Rick Astley is a British singer and songwriter whose name became synonymous with one of the internet's most iconic memes.
But long before the digital fame, he was a pop sensation with a voice that surprised the world.
Born on February 6, 1966, in Lancashire, England, Rick Astley shot to fame in 1987 with his debut single,
“Never Gonna Give You Up.” The track became a global hit, reaching #1 in 25 countries and making Rick an overnight star.
His deep, soulful voice was an unexpected contrast to his youthful, clean-cut look — and that contrast became part of his appeal.
